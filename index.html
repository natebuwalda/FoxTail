Please excuse the mess!

  This website is far from 'live' and ready, but enough people wanted to see how it's coming, as well as the jewelry being worked on, so here it is, in it's unfinished incarnation!

 

  Welcome to the home of Foxtail Artisanry.  Most of the gallery of items shown will list estimated prices, and send you to Etsy pages if I have pieces of that type in stock.  Unless otherwise noted, everything in the Gallery can be ordered and made specifically for you, just contact us!

  Currently, jewelry is being offered in copper, bronze, brass, stainless steel, and many colors of anodized aluminum.  Silver and gold, as well as anodized niobium and titanium are available by special order.  Likewise, other chainmail weaves and crystals are available by special order.  All payments for non-Etsy items will be via Paypal.  We will ship out of the US, via flat-rate boxes from USPS.
