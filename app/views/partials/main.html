Welcome to the home of Foxtail Artisanry.

  This little place shows the work of our whole household, working together to make fine handmade jewelry, clothing, and other things, one bit at a time. Every piece of chainmaille is done one ring at a time with careful attention given to the work. Don't be shy about emailing in if you have interest in any of the pieces or want something made specially for you out of this or that weave but done in a different size or style. Saw a bracelet you loved, but couldn't find it as a necklace? Just ask, we can probably make it!

  Most of the gallery of items shown will have links to Etsy, Goodsmith, or even Square marketplace pages if we have pieces of that type up on it, though sometimes there is stock we'll have that won't be up on them. If you can't find what you want on those three, definitely check in here..   Unless otherwise noted, everything in the Gallery can be ordered and made specifically for you in various colors or materials, including those not in the pictures, just contact us.

  Currently, jewellery is being offered in copper, bronze, brass, stainless steel, and many colors of anodized aluminium.  Silver and gold, as well as anodized niobium and titanium are available by special order.  Likewise, other chainmaille weaves and crystals are available by special order.  All payments for non-Etsy items will be via Paypal.  We will ship out of the US, via flat-rate boxes from USPS, though you'll need to talk to us first to help make sure exactly what the prices will be.
