  Pardon the fact that everything is currently 'in one place'.  We're still expanding what items we have properly photographed, and didn't want to put people in tiny little one item galleries!

  Unless otherwise noted, everything in the Gallery can be ordered and made specifically for you in various colors or materials, including those not in the pictures, just contact us.

  Length of item can be changed.  If it's a small change, it won't affect the price, but larger changes mean extra work and materials and thusly will change the price.  And yes, a dramatic shortening will lower the price!  It'd be unfair to charge for work or materials that aren't actually happening, after all.  Change of clasps is also an option, and likely will not affect the price, unless dealing in the more expensive materials.  Stainless Steel, titanium, niobium, silver, gold, etc.  It's important to have the kind of closure you want, as opposed to one you will dislike and lead you to rarely wearing the handmade jewelry.  And if you're worried about the hassle of closing lobster claw clasps (our preferred clasp, since it won't just 'slip' open and fall off!), we've got a little chain to help assist in putting them on one handed for the bracelets.
