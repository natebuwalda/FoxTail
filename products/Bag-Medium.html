Bag - Medium size

  The 'Standard' size of our dicebag, holding approx. 50 dice of normal size. Two colors, 14g AWG/1.6mm rings, leather cord and cord stop.

  Pictured above, anodized aluminum in two colors. Three colors is also an option for the price, as are other designs, though four or more colors would increase the price.

  While it's also available in copper, bronze, and brass, those metals tarnish and won't be as nice looking in the long term. Stainless steel is viable, but is quite a bit heavier, as well as being much more expensive for the jeweler's cut of rings (saw cut as opposed to machine cut or hand cut with snips.) You might wonder not use the much cheaper machine cut? The edges and imperfections will very quickly scratch up the dice inside, and we don't want that to happen!
